Lung Cancer Research Study | May 2022  


John Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory: MATLAB II Final Project

A study looking at lung cancer. The program estimates the global smoking-to-lung-cancer rate using data from the real world... I used two datasets: one that represented global smoking rates and the other that represented the number of smokers who also had lung cancer. Using the data from these datasets, I estimated the average smoking-to-lung cancer rate and applied it to the top five smoking countries globally as well as to Asian and European countries. Bar graphs were used to display this data.

Quiz Program | Decemeber 2021  


John Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory: MATLAB I Final Project

A MATLAB-created game that tests your knowledge of math, science, history, english, and technology. After that, it scores your responses and provides you with a score as well as a graph that displays your performance in each subject... The program quizzed the user using the MATLAB console. These were a series of pre-programmed questions stored in an array. The program checked the answers to the pre-programmed answers array when the quiz was finished. This information was presented on a bar graph and a box plot.
